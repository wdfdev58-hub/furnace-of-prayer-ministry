Our vision

The fire on the altar must never go out.

Furnace of Prayer Ministry exists to keep that flame burning — a people continually before the Lord, contending in prayer until heaven touches earth and hearts are turned back to Jesus.

Under the leadership of Pastor Mpontshang Princess Latha, we are an ordinary people who have met an extraordinary God — gathering in Kagung, Kuruman to seek His face.
